How Local Businesses Can Use AI to Get More Customers

Published May 26, 2026 | By AIToolPilot Team

Local businesses are the backbone of every community, but they face unique challenges: limited marketing budgets, fierce competition, and the need to stand out online and offline. In 2026, AI tools are leveling the playing field, helping local businesses attract more customers without breaking the bank.

Here’s how local businesses can use AI to get more customers, with practical tools for every step:

1. Durable – AI Website Builder

The Problem: Many local businesses lack a professional website or struggle to keep it updated.

The Solution: Durable uses AI to build and update your website in minutes, with built-in SEO and lead capture.

How to Use: Answer a few questions about your business, and Durable generates a ready-to-launch site.

Affiliate Link: Try Durable

2. SurferSEO – Local SEO

The Problem: Getting found on Google is critical for local businesses.

The Solution: SurferSEO helps you optimize your website and Google Business Profile for local search.

How to Use: Run a local SEO audit, follow Surfer’s recommendations, and track your rankings.

Affiliate Link: Try SurferSEO

3. Jasper AI – Content & Reviews

The Problem: Local businesses need fresh content and positive reviews to build trust.

The Solution: Jasper AI generates blog posts, social media updates, and even review responses.

How to Use: Use Jasper’s templates for local content and automate review follow-ups.

Affiliate Link: Try Jasper AI

4. Pictory – Video Marketing

The Problem: Video content drives engagement, but production is expensive.

The Solution: Pictory turns your text and images into professional videos for social media and ads.

How to Use: Upload your content, select a style, and let Pictory create your video.

Affiliate Link: Try Pictory

5. Zapier – Automate Customer Follow-Up

The Problem: Following up with leads and customers is time-consuming.

The Solution: Zapier automates follow-up emails, review requests, and appointment reminders.

How to Use: Set up Zaps to connect your website, CRM, and email tools.

Affiliate Link: Try Zapier
