The $100/Month AI Stack That Replaces a $5,000/Month Team

The exact tools, setup, and workflows a solo founder can use to handle content, design, automation, and SEO — for under $100/month.

The Problem This Solves

A typical small business needs: a content writer, a graphic designer, a social media manager, an SEO specialist, and someone to handle repetitive admin tasks. Hiring even part-time help for all of this costs $3,000–$8,000/month. Most solo founders and small business owners can't afford that.

In 2026, a well-chosen set of AI tools can cover 70–80% of these functions for under $100/month. Here's the exact stack.

The Stack (Total: ~$88/month)

AI Writing — Copy.ai Pro ($39/month)

Handles: blog post drafts, social media captions, email copy, product descriptions, ad copy.

Why Copy.ai over Jasper: At this budget, Copy.ai's Pro plan ($39/month) gives unlimited words and covers most writing needs. Upgrade to Jasper ($49/month) when you need long-form content at higher volume.

Read our Copy.ai review →

AI Design — Canva Pro ($15/month)

Handles: social media graphics, presentation decks, email headers, blog thumbnails, ad creatives.

The Magic Resize feature alone saves 2–3 hours per week by automatically reformatting designs for every platform. Brand Kit ensures everything looks consistent without a designer.

Read our Canva Pro review →

AI Automation — Make Core ($9/month)

Handles: connecting your tools, automating repetitive tasks, syncing data between apps.

Example automations to set up on day one:

  • New blog post published → auto-post to social media
  • New email subscriber → add to CRM + send welcome sequence
  • New sale → update spreadsheet + send Slack notification
  • Weekly → pull analytics data into a Google Sheet dashboard

Read our Make review →

AI Website — Durable ($15/month)

Handles: your business website, contact forms, basic SEO.

If you already have a website, skip this. If you don't, Durable gets you live in 30 minutes with a professional result. Upgrade to a more customizable platform (Webflow, WordPress) when you outgrow it.

Read our Durable review →

Free Tools to Round Out the Stack

  • ChatGPT Free: Brainstorming, research, quick rewrites
  • Google Analytics 4: Website traffic and conversion tracking
  • Google Search Console: SEO performance monitoring
  • Notion Free: Content calendar and project management
  • Mailchimp Free: Email list up to 500 subscribers

Monthly Cost Breakdown

  • Copy.ai Pro: $39
  • Canva Pro: $15
  • Make Core: $9
  • Durable Starter: $15
  • Everything else: $0
  • Total: $78/month

What This Stack Can't Replace

Be honest about the limits. This stack doesn't replace:

  • Strategic thinking and business judgment
  • Deep subject matter expertise in your content
  • Relationship-building and sales conversations
  • Complex custom development work
  • High-production video (use Descript or Pictory for basic video)

How to Start

  1. Sign up for Canva Pro first — it has the fastest ROI and lowest learning curve
  2. Add Copy.ai and spend one week learning its templates
  3. Set up Make with 3 automations that save you the most time
  4. Add Durable only if you need a new website

Don't try to implement everything at once. Add one tool per week, learn it properly, then add the next.
