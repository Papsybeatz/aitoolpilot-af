Overview

Pictory is an AI video creation platform that converts written content — blog posts, scripts, articles — into polished short-form videos. It automatically selects relevant stock footage, adds captions, and syncs audio. For businesses that want to repurpose content into video without hiring a video editor, Pictory is the fastest path.

Key Features

  • Article to Video: Paste a URL or text and Pictory builds a video automatically
  • Script to Video: Turn any script into a narrated video with stock footage
  • Auto Captions: AI-generated captions for accessibility and social media
  • Brand Kit: Add your logo, colors, and fonts to every video
  • AI Voiceover: 40+ AI voices in multiple languages
  • Video Highlights: Extract short clips from long videos automatically
  • 3M+ Stock Library: Licensed footage, images, and music included

Who Is It For?

  • Bloggers who want to repurpose articles into YouTube or social videos
  • Content marketers producing short-form video at scale
  • Coaches and consultants turning webinar recordings into clips
  • Small businesses without a video editing team
