Overview

Make (formerly Integromat) is a powerful visual automation platform that connects apps and automates workflows. With over 1,000+ apps and advanced filtering, it offers more flexibility than Zapier for complex scenarios.

Key Features

  • Visual Scenario Builder: Drag-and-drop editor for complex workflows
  • 1,000+ App Integrations: Extensive app library with advanced triggers
  • Router & Filters: Advanced branching logic and data transformation
  • Error Handling: Built-in error handling and execution history
  • Unlimited Scenarios: No restriction on complexity on paid plans
