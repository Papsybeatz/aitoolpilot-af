AI copywriting assistant for marketing teams who need speed without the premium price

Overview

Copy.ai is an AI writing tool built specifically for marketing copy, social posts, and sales content. It offers a generous free plan, 90+ templates for common copywriting tasks, and an Infobase feature that lets you store brand knowledge for consistent output. It's a strong alternative to Jasper for teams with tighter budgets.

Key Features

  • 90+ Templates: Sales copy, social media, product descriptions, email sequences
  • Chat & Editor: Two modes — conversational AI and a structured document editor
  • Brand Voice: Train the AI on your brand guidelines and tone
  • Infobase: Store product info, company facts, and FAQs for consistent output
  • Team Collaboration: Share prompts, templates, and generated content
  • Workflows: Multi-step automated content pipelines

Who Is It For?

  • Solo founders and freelancers who need fast marketing copy
  • Small marketing teams producing social and email content at scale
  • E-commerce businesses needing product descriptions quickly
  • Agencies looking for a cost-effective Jasper alternative

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Generous free plan to test before buying
  • Infobase keeps output on-brand automatically
  • Fast for short-form copy (ads, social, emails)
  • Cleaner, simpler UI than Jasper
  • Affordable Pro plan for small teams

Cons

  • Weaker for long-form content vs. Jasper
  • No native SEO integration
  • Workflows can feel limited on lower plans
  • Output quality varies by template

How We Use Copy.ai at AIToolPilot

We use Copy.ai for rapid social media caption drafts, email subject line testing, and ad copy variations. For long-form blog content, we prefer Jasper — but for anything under 500 words, Copy.ai is faster and cheaper.
