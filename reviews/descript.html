Overview

Descript is a video and podcast editor that works like a word processor. It transcribes your recording, then lets you edit the audio and video by editing the text transcript. Delete a word from the transcript and it disappears from the video. It also includes AI overdub (clone your voice), filler word removal, and screen recording — making it the most complete content creation suite for non-editors.

Key Features

  • Text-Based Editing: Edit audio/video by editing the transcript — no timeline required
  • AI Overdub: Clone your voice to fix mistakes without re-recording
  • Filler Word Removal: Auto-remove "um", "uh", and silence with one click
  • Screen Recording: Built-in screen and webcam recorder
  • Auto Captions: Accurate captions with custom styling
  • Clip Creation: Extract social clips from long recordings
  • Collaboration: Share projects and leave comments like Google Docs

Who Is It For?

  • Podcasters who want to edit episodes without audio engineering skills
  • Course creators recording tutorials and walkthroughs
  • Video marketers producing YouTube and social content
  • Teams creating internal training videos
  • Anyone who finds traditional video editors intimidating
