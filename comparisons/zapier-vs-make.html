Our Recommendation

Choose Zapier if:

  • You want the widest selection of app integrations
  • You prefer a simple, step-by-step interface
  • You need reliable, proven automation with minimal setup
  • You're new to automation and want the most user-friendly option
  • You need extensive community support and templates

Choose Make if:

  • You need to build complex workflows with branching logic
  • You prefer a visual, flowchart-style interface
  • You want more operations for your money (especially on free tier)
  • You need advanced error handling and data manipulation
  • You're comfortable with a moderate learning curve for more power

Bottom Line: Zapier is better for beginners and those who need the most app integrations. Make is better for users who need complex visual workflows and want more value for their money.
