Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

What This Means

AIToolPilot participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click certain links on this site and make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links on this site are marked with rel="sponsored" in the HTML and typically include UTM parameters such as ?utm_source=aitoolpilot&utm_medium=affiliate. Buttons labeled "Try [Tool]" or styled as orange call-to-action buttons are affiliate links.

We also use affiliate links in our newsletter and blog posts. We will always disclose when a link is an affiliate link.

How This Affects Our Reviews

Our editorial policy is to review tools honestly regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship with the vendor. We do not accept payment for positive reviews. We do not inflate ratings for tools that pay higher commissions.

We review tools we have actually used. Our ratings reflect our genuine assessment of each tool's value for small businesses.

If a tool we review does not have an affiliate program, we still review it if it's relevant to our audience. The absence of an affiliate link does not indicate a negative opinion.
